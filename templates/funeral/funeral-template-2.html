HONORING THE LIFE OF

Michael James

1954 — 2026

A Legacy of Kindness

"My father didn't teach me how to live; he lived, and let me watch him do it. He was a man of few words but infinite actions. Every morning, I remember the smell of fresh coffee and the sound of his old radio humming in the workshop—a sound that meant safety, stability, and love.

He taught me that the measure of a life isn't found in the titles we hold, but in the quiet moments we share with those who matter most. I carry his legacy in every step I take, hoping to make him as proud as I am to be his son."
